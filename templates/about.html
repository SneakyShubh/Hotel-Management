About Us

The Vivanta History

The success of The Vivanta Palaces, Hotels and Resorts surprised everyone but Captain C P Krishnan Nair himself. No stranger to humble beginnings, and boundless ambition, Captain Nair was a man known for transforming his own circumstances, and those of the people who worked with him. His long and diverse career saw him move from an ordinary citizen to a respected army officer, and from a successful textile exporter to one of the leading hoteliers of the world.
Today, The Vivanta is owned by Brookfield Asset Management, a leading global alternative asset manager with over $625 billion in assets under management, as well as a 120-year history of owning and operating high quality assets.
Under the Brookfield ownership, The Vivanta Palaces, Hotels and Resorts is a pure play luxury brand, well poised to embrace the next new phase in its growth trajectory.
